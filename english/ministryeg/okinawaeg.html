Okinawa Staffs　　　　　　　　　　　　　 DTS

YWAM Okinawa started full-time activities in 1988. We focus on Discipleship Training (DTS) which is one of YWAM's three pillars. Our DTS is 6 months. We also equip God's saints through Mini-DTS (3 months), various seminars, open meetings, retreats, etc. We participate in local area evangelism, coffee house evangelism, and English conversation evangelism. We serve the local churches and support inter-denominational meetings to advance God's kingdom. We hope, pray, and intercede for the expansion of our service areas.
